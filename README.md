## Hi there 👋
# Hi, I'm Amith Balasubramanya 👋

Biomedical Engineer | Computational Biomechanics | Fluid-Structure Interaction

I work at the intersection of **biomedical engineering, fluid dynamics, and computational modeling**, using simulations to better understand cardiovascular mechanics and medical devices.

My research focuses on **fluid-solid interaction (FSI), hemodynamics, and biomechanical modeling** with applications in cardiovascular disease and medical device design.

## 🔬 Research Interests

- Cardiovascular biomechanics
- Fluid–Structure Interaction (FSI)
- Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
- Biofluid Mechanics
- Biomedical device design and simulation
